## Description

Based on Week 6 Example 5 code, I updated the sprite sheets for the player, enemy, "leaf", tileset, and more, in order to change the look of the level. I also added sounds for jumping, attacking, taking damage, collecting objects, and added a looping background track.

## Setup and Interaction Instructions

The player travels across platforms to "rescue" all of the magical keys. They are able to press either WASD or the arrow keys in order to run across the screen or jump. The player can also press the spacebar in order to attack the enemy. Once the enemy is hit a certain number of times, they will disappear, making it easier to collect the keys. If the player comes in touch with the fire or the enemy (without attacking in time), they will receive damage, losing a heart. The player has 3 hearts in total. Once all 3 hearts are used up, the game ends. The player can press R to restart the game.
